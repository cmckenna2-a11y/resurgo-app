Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

Resurgo is a student wellness app. We are not a healthcare provider, therapist, or clinical service.

2. What data we collect

We collect your name, email address, and password when you create an account. We collect your mood check-ins, journal entries, onboarding answers, and any stories you submit. We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or microphone.

3. How we store your data

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Your rights

You can update your personal information in the You tab at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

6. Minimum age

Resurgo is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

7. Crisis disclaimer

Resurgo is not a crisis service. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
